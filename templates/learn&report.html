🧠 Learn & Report

✨ Why This Project Exists

AwareLock was built to protect everyday users from today’s online scams in India. As fraud tactics evolve, education is the strongest defense. This platform simulates real cyber frauds so you can recognize and avoid them in real life.

💡 How This Helps You
  • 🔍 Spot red flags in real time
  • 🧠 Understand how scammers trick users
  • 🛡️ Build safe online habits

Your interactions contribute to an Awareness Score, showing how ready you are to face real threats. ✅

⚙️ How AwareLock Works
  1. 📂 Select a Module – Choose from simulations like phishing, fake delivery, or investment scams.
  2. 🎭 Experience a Realistic Scam – Interact with messages, links, and calls that mimic real fraud tactics.
  3. 👀 Learn to Identify Red Flags – The system shows you what’s suspicious and why it’s dangerous.
  4. 📊 Track Your Awareness Score – Each choice you make improves your learning progress.
  5. 🚨 Apply in Real Life – Use this knowledge to stay safe and report fraud attempts instantly.
🔒 Trust & Safety Points
  • ✅ No real malware — 100% safe simulations
  • ✅ We never store your passwords or financial details
  • ✅ Your progress stays private — you decide what to share
  • ✅ Encrypted connection (HTTPS/TLS) for every visit
  • ✅ Clear feedback after every exercise —eg. learn why it’s phishing/fraud
  • ✅ Sample data only — Please never use your real credentials
🚨 Take Action If You’ve Faced this type of Scam
  • 🛑 Do not ignore it.
  • 📞 Report immediately to:
    • 🔗 Cyber Crime Portal
    • 🔗 RBI Sachet Portal
  • 📢 Your report could prevent the next victim.
